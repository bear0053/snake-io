How to Play

  • Use Arrow Keys / WASD, swipe, or the on-screen D-pad to steer your snake.
  • Eat food to grow and score points. Avoid walls, yourself, and dangerous obstacles.
  • Golden food is worth big points. Poison food hurts you — avoid it!
  • Grab power-ups for shields, magnets, and double points.
  • Complete a level's objective to unlock the next level and earn stars.
  • Unlock new snake skins by completing levels.
